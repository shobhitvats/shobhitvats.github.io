Who I Am

I'm Shobhit — a final-year BS-MS student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Pune, specialising in Computational Biology. My work lives at the intersection of rigorous science and pragmatic engineering.

Over four years at IISER, I've developed a research identity that spans structural bioinformatics, HPC pipeline optimization, and the development of full-stack scientific software tools. In parallel, I've had the privilege of applying these skills in industry — automating validation workflows at Velsera and evaluating large language models at Outlier.

I believe the best solutions emerge when deep domain knowledge meets thoughtful engineering — and I'm constantly working to deepen both. Whether I'm building a GPU-accelerated molecular mechanics engine or a zero-API-cost intelligence platform, the core philosophy is the same: understand the problem deeply, then engineer an honest solution.

Outside the lab and the terminal, I'm a musician (flute, guitar, keyboard), a digital artist, a chess enthusiast, and someone who genuinely enjoys mentoring and team building. I've led large-scale institutional events, coordinated science and art clubs, and been part of the entrepreneurship ecosystem at IISER — because good ideas deserve good execution at every level.

Research

Academic research conducted at IISER Pune

● Ongoing · Masters Research Dec 2025 – Present

Pnictogen & Tetrel Bonding in Protein Structures

Investigating the role of pnictogen (N, P, As) and tetrel (C, Si, Ge) bonding interactions in protein structure and function. The study derives geometric parameters from CSD crystal structures and validates them against PDB protein data, culminating in web-based analysis tools integrated into MolBridge.

KEY ASPECTS
  • CSD-derived geometric parameter benchmarking
  • Large-scale PDB structural validation
  • Web-based analysis via MolBridge integration
  • Statistical analysis of interaction geometry
IISER Pune — Sai Lab Prof. Saikrishnan Kayarat
Completed Jan–May 2025

Chalcogen Interactions in Protein Structures

Designed and built a utility application for the analysis and identification of chalcogen (S, Se, Te) interactions in protein structures using geometric criteria from the CSD. Generated the MolBridge chalcogen detection module.

Sai LabCSDPDB
Completed Aug–Dec 2024

Torsion Angle Analysis & Ramachandran Plots

Conformational analysis of torsion angles (φ, ψ) and Ramachandran plot visualization for peptide motifs. Leveraged concurrent programming for large-scale data analysis across thousands of PDB files.

Sai LabPDBHPC
Completed Aug 2023

NMR Analysis of Biomolecules

Hands-on NMR analysis of proteins, protein purification, and product synthesis for NMR characterization at Dr. Jeetendra Chug's lab (JC Lab).
